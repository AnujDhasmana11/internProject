Aam Shrikhand/Aamras

  • 2 cups plain yogurt
  • 1 cup mango pulp
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts for garnishing

Instructions:

  1. Hang the yogurt in a muslin cloth for 2-3 hours to remove excess water.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine hung yogurt, mango pulp, powdered sugar, and cardamom powder. Mix well.
  3. Refrigerate the mixture for an hour before serving.
  4. Garnish with chopped nuts before serving.
