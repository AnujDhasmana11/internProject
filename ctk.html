Chicken Tangdi Kebab without Tandoor/Oven

  • 500 grams chicken drumsticks (tangdi)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil

Instructions:

  1. Clean and wash the chicken drumsticks. Make deep gashes on them.
  2. In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, red chili powder, garam masala, cumin powder, coriander powder, chopped coriander leaves, and salt to form a marinade.
  3. Coat the chicken drumsticks with the marinade. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight.
  4. Heat oil in a non-stick pan. Place the marinated chicken drumsticks in the pan.
  5. Cook on medium heat for about 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with mint chutney and onion rings.
