High Protein Chana Chaat

  • 2 cups boiled chickpeas (chana)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 cucumber, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon chaat masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon roasted cumin powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine boiled chickpeas, chopped onion, tomato, cucumber, coriander leaves, and green chili.
  2. Add chaat masala, roasted cumin powder, red chili powder, lemon juice, and salt. Mix well.
  3. Adjust seasoning according to taste.
  4. Serve chilled as a healthy snack.
