Green Salad Vegetable and Fruit Combination

  • 2 cups mixed salad greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula, etc.)
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup sliced strawberries
  • 1/4 cup blueberries
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinaigrette dressing

Instructions:

  1. In a large salad bowl, combine mixed salad greens, cucumber, bell pepper, cherry tomatoes, strawberries, blueberries, feta cheese, and walnuts.
  2. Toss the salad ingredients gently to mix.
  3. Drizzle balsamic vinaigrette dressing over the salad and toss again until well coated.
  4. Serve immediately as a refreshing side dish or appetizer.
