Dahi Wali Masala Bhindi

  • 500 grams okra (bhindi), washed and dried
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons gram flour (besan)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil

Instructions:

  1. Cut the okra into halves or slices and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, whisk yogurt with gram flour until smooth. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well to form a marinade.
  3. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, and asafoetida. Let them crackle.
  4. Add the okra slices to the pan and saut√© for a few minutes.
  5. Pour the yogurt marinade over the okra and mix well. Cover and cook for 10-15 minutes on low heat until the okra is tender.
  6. Once done, garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or rice.
