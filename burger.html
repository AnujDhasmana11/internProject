Burger Recipe

  • 1/4 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup minced onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Optional toppings: lettuce, tomato, cheese, pickles, ketchup, mustard

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties. Season each patty with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  4. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheese on each patty and allow it to melt.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in the skillet for about 1 minute.
  6. Assemble your burgers by placing lettuce leaves, tomato slices, onion slices, and pickles (if using) on the bottom half of each bun.
  7. Place the cooked patties with melted cheese on top of the vegetables.
  8. Spread ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise on the top half of each bun.
  9. Place the top half of the bun on the patty to complete the burger.

Pizza Recipe

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced onions
  • 1/4 cup sliced olives
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature setting (usually around 475°F or 245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface into your desired shape (round, rectangular, etc.).
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Spread a layer of pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Add your chosen toppings evenly over the cheese.
  7. Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  8. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
